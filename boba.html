Boba liberalism, a term first coined by twitter user @diaspora_is_red. Liberation and identity politics are falsely equated with commodification and consumerism. We associated boba, Hello Kitty, Pocky, etc with Asian American identity more than the radical origins behind the term “Asian American”.

Asian American liberalism is not anti-capitalist or anti-imperialist. It’s insidiousness lies within in it’s complacency with its place in the imperial core and it’s disconnection from transnational solidarity.
