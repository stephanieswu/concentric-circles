A common experience from people of the diaspora is feeling like you're in limbo between the homeland and your Westernized existence, not fitting neatly into either side.

For me, being Chinese American becomes an oxymoron. The American media that I am supposed to consume continues to push for Sinophobic narratives and, while I know that these stories are highly biased toward American imperialist interests, my distance from China itself leaves me wavering.
